Free Online Image Resizer — Resize Any Photo in Seconds

Our free online image resizer allows you to change the dimensions of any image directly in your browser. Whether you're preparing images for social media, resizing product photos for an e-commerce store, or creating the perfect passport photo, this tool does it all — instantly and without any software installation.

Choose from a range of popular presets for Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, Twitter, and passport photos, or enter custom width and height values. The built-in aspect ratio lock ensures your images stay proportional, preventing stretching or distortion.

All processing happens 100% in your browser — your images are never uploaded to any server. This means maximum privacy and near-instant results, regardless of your internet speed. You can also convert between JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats and control the output quality for optimal file size.

Properly sized images are critical for web performance and SEO. Search engines favor fast-loading pages, and oversized images are a top cause of slow page speeds. Use our resizer to ensure every image on your site is perfectly optimized for its container.

Frequently Asked Questions

You can resize JPEG, PNG, and WebP images. You can also convert between these formats during the resize process — for example, upload a PNG and download a smaller WebP version.

Instagram square posts should be 1080 × 1080 pixels. Portrait posts perform best at 1080 × 1350, and Stories should be 1080 × 1920. All of these presets are built into our tool for one-click resizing.

Enlarging images beyond their original resolution may reduce sharpness. Downsizing retains quality well. Use the quality slider to control the output — higher values produce better quality but larger files.

When the aspect ratio lock is enabled (the lock icon is closed), changing the width automatically adjusts the height proportionally, and vice versa. This prevents your image from being stretched or squished. Disable it if you need non-proportional dimensions.

Standard US passport photos are 2 × 2 inches (51 × 51 mm). At 300 DPI, this translates to approximately 413 × 531 pixels. Our passport preset uses these exact dimensions.
