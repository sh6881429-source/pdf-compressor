Free Online Image Compressor — Fast, Private & Lossless

Our free online image compressor lets you reduce the file size of your images in seconds — directly in your browser. Whether you need to optimize photos for your website, shrink images for email attachments, or prepare visuals for social media, this tool handles it all without compromising quality.

Unlike other tools that upload your files to remote servers, our compressor processes everything locally on your device. Your images never leave your browser, ensuring complete privacy and security. There are no watermarks, no sign-ups, and no file limits.

The tool supports all major image formats including JPEG, PNG, and WebP. Use the quality slider to fine-tune the compression level — from maximum file size reduction to near-lossless output. You can also convert between formats on the fly, choosing WebP for maximum web performance or JPEG for universal compatibility.

Image compression is essential for website performance and SEO. Google's Core Web Vitals penalize slow-loading pages, and oversized images are the #1 cause of sluggish load times. By compressing your images before uploading them to your site, you can improve page speed scores, reduce bandwidth costs, and deliver a better user experience.
