Privacy Policy

1. No File Storage Policy

At FreeTools, your privacy is our highest priority. Unlike many online tools, we operate under a strict "No File Storage" policy. All image and PDF processing is performed locally in your web browser using JavaScript and WebAssembly.

This means your files are never uploaded to our servers. The data stays entirely on your device during the entire process.

2. Files Auto-Deleted

Since your files are processed client-side, they only exist in your browser's temporary memory. As soon as you close the browser tab or refresh the page, all file data is automatically cleared by the browser. We do not maintain any cache or permanent storage of your documents or images.

3. No Personal Data Collection

We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal information to use our tools. We do not track individual users or build profiles. Our tools are designed to be completely anonymous.

4. Use of Cookies & Advertising

To keep our tools free for everyone, we may show advertisements via services like Google AdSense. These services may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites on the Internet.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@freetools.dev.
