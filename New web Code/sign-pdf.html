Free PDF Signer — Add Electronic Signatures Safely

Need to sign a contract, NDA, or official form? Our free online PDF Signer allows you to legally bind electronic signatures to your PDF documents instantly. You don't need to print, sign, and scan your documents anymore, preserving 100% of the original document's vector quality.

You can create your signature in two ways: draw it naturally using your mouse, trackpad, or touchscreen, or simply upload a pre-existing image of your signature. Once created, just drag the signature to the correct signature line and click download!

Total Privacy and Security: We understand that documents requiring a signature are often highly confidential. That is why our PDF signer operates entirely offline within your browser. Your files are never uploaded to a server, ensuring no third party can ever access your private contracts or sensitive data.
