Free PDF Compressor — Shrink Files Without Losing Quality

Large PDF files can be a hassle to deal with. They bounce back from email servers, take forever to upload, and consume valuable storage space. Our free online PDF compressor solves this problem by drastically reducing the size of your documents while maintaining read-ability and visual quality.

We use advanced optimization techniques to shrink your PDFs. This includes restructuring the document, stripping out redundant metadata, and consolidating fonts and resources. The result is a much lighter file that is perfectly optimized for the web and email.

Security and Privacy First: Unlike other PDF tools that force you to upload sensitive documents to their servers, our tool uses client-side JavaScript. This means the entire compression process happens locally in your web browser. Your PDF never leaves your device, ensuring complete confidentiality for your personal, legal, or business documents.
