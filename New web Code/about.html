Your Toolkit for a Faster, More Private Web.

We've built a suite of essential image and PDF tools that work entirely in your browser. No accounts, no uploads, no compromises.

The Problem with Modern Tools

Most "free" online tools require you to upload your sensitive documents and private photos to their servers. This slow process wastes bandwidth and, more importantly, exposes your data to third-party storage and potential breaches.

We believed there was a better way to build for the modern web using the power of your own computer.

Our Local-First Solution

FreeTools uses cutting-edge browser technologies to process your files locally on your own CPU and GPU. Whether you're compressing a PDF or removing an image background, the data never leaves your device.

The result? Lightning-fast performance, zero data usage, and absolute privacy by design.

Privacy First

We don't want your data. All processing happens in your browser's sandbox, ensuring your files are safe and private.

No Waiting

Skip the upload and download bars. Experience near-instant processing speeds powered by your own hardware.

Always Free

No subscriptions or hidden fees. We rely on subtle advertising to keep the lights on while providing professional tools.
