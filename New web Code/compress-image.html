Free Online Image Compressor — Reduce Size Without Losing Quality

Need to shrink your photos for a website, email attachment, or social media post? Our free image compressor makes it effortless. Upload any JPG, PNG, or WebP image — up to a generous 200 MB — and watch the file size drop in seconds. Unlike other tools, we never upload your images to a remote server. All compression runs entirely inside your web browser using the native Canvas API, which means your files stay completely private and secure.

Use the quality slider for fine-grained control, or pick a preset — Low for maximum compression, Medium for the best balance, or High for near-lossless results. You can even specify an exact target file size in KB and the tool will automatically find the right quality level to hit your goal. The live Before/After slider lets you compare the original and compressed images side-by-side, so you'll always know exactly what you're getting before you download.

Whether you're a photographer optimizing portfolio images, a developer improving page load speed, or just someone who needs to fit a photo under an upload limit, this tool has you covered. It works on every modern browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge — on desktop and mobile alike.

Tips for Best Results

  • JPEG at 70–80% quality delivers the best size-to-quality ratio for photos.
  • WebP format produces the smallest files — 25-35% smaller than JPEG at equal quality.
  • PNG is lossless — quality slider has minimal effect. Convert to JPEG or WebP for dramatic savings.
  • Use the Target Size option when you need to meet a specific upload limit (e.g. 100 KB for a forum avatar).
