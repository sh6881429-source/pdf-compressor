Secure Your PDFs Locally

Whether you need to share sensitive financial records, legal contracts, or personal documents, our PDF Security Suite ensures your files remain completely private. Lock your PDFs with a strong password to prevent unauthorized access.

Received a password-protected PDF that you're tired of entering the password for? Use our Unlock PDF feature to permanently remove the password restrictions. Please note that you must know the current password to unlock the document; we do not provide password cracking services.

How to Lock a PDF

  1. Select the "Lock PDF" tab at the top.
  2. Upload your PDF file.
  3. Enter and confirm a strong password.
  4. Click "Protect PDF" and download your newly encrypted file.

How to Unlock a PDF

  1. Select the "Unlock PDF" tab at the top.
  2. Upload your password-protected PDF.
  3. Enter the correct password.
  4. Click "Unlock PDF" and download the restriction-free file.
